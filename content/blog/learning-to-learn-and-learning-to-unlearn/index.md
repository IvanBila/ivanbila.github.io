## Tornar a mente permeavel, mudar sempre que dever

> I'm frequently trying to benchmark opinions i have against alternatives to see if they still hold. this is particularly so for technology, but applies broadly as well. It's exhilarating to try to invalidade your current operating theses, because if you can, there's a leap to make. I've made many such leaps. DHH
